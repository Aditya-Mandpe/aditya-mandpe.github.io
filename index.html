About

Creative leader.
Operations thinker.
AI practitioner.

I started in video production houses and ad agencies in Hyderabad and Mumbai, learning the craft of making things for brands at scale. I moved to Bengaluru in 2020 to join Myntra and spent the last five years building something I did not have a roadmap for: a fully AI-powered visual production operation inside one of India's largest consumer platforms.

My work sits at the intersection of creative direction and operational thinking. I care as much about what a piece of content does to a conversion metric as I do about whether it is visually honest and well-crafted. Those two things are not in conflict.

I have built teams, deprecated broken processes, introduced AI at every layer of production, and maintained quality at 20,000 videos a month. I am now looking for a new challenge where both the creative and operational ambition are high.
