Myntra · Strategy & Production

The AI Pivot

From a single GPT plugin to 90% AI video production at scale

Legacy video production was shoot-dependent, slow, and selection-limited. We saw an opportunity to change that from the ground up — ingesting AI into every layer of the workflow, replacing manual processes with scalable pipelines, and shifting from physical shoots to AI-generated output. Today 90% of all catalog video and imagery runs on AI. The remaining 10% is deliberate: fabric and product detail content where physical texture still drives stronger conversion.

Social Styled AI Videos

The highest-performing AI video format in Myntra catalog. Launched Nov 2025.

Catalog videos were functional. They were not relatable. Social Styled AI Videos were built to fix that: real-looking people in real settings, across different body types and ethnicities, generated entirely with AI. No shoot, no casting, no scheduling. Launched November 2025 and immediately became the top-performing video format in the catalog.

AI Imagery at Scale

Rebuilding the catalog imagery pipeline from the ground up

About

Creative leader.
Operations thinker.
AI practitioner.

I started in video production houses and ad agencies in Hyderabad and Mumbai, learning the craft of making things for brands at scale. I moved to Bengaluru in 2020 to join Myntra and spent the last five years building something I did not have a roadmap for: a fully AI-powered visual production operation inside one of India's largest consumer platforms.

My work sits at the intersection of creative direction and operational thinking. I care as much about what a piece of content does to a conversion metric as I do about whether it is visually honest and well-crafted. Those two things are not in conflict.

I have built teams, deprecated broken processes, introduced AI at every layer of production, and maintained quality at 20,000 videos a month. I am now looking for a new challenge where both the creative and operational ambition are high.
